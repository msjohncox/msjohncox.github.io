Form & function follow relevance

No matter how complex or beautiful it appears, a Website should not draw attention to its existence. It must draw all its qualities from its subject, becoming an extension of the very thing it is about.

If the Website faithfully reveals its subject's qualities and emotions, then the beauty of its design and pleasure of its function will come naturally.

This is very hard to accomplish.
